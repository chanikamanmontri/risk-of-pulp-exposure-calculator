⚠️ Clinical Disclaimer

This calculator is a decision-support tool for preoperative planning and communication — it does not prescribe treatment selection. The predicted probability should be used to guide clinician preparedness, material preparation, and informed consent discussions. The extent of carious tissue removal, choice of restorative material, and type of vital pulp therapy remain dictated by intraoperative findings, pulp tissue status, and established operative principles. Regardless of predicted risk, all treated teeth require standardised long-term clinical and radiographic follow-up.
